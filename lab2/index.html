Installing the Pear Deck Add-On in Slides

Jorey C. McComas

Step 1: Open New Google Slide Project

There are several ways to open Google Slides. The way shown here is to type slides.google.com in a browser search bar. Feel free to use any method you prefer to open a new Google Slides project.


Step 2: Start a New Presentation
Select a blank to open a new presentation. Alternatively, you can open an existing presentation to set up any add-on as well.

Step 3: Select the Add-ons menu
Find the Add-ons menu in the menu bar near the top of the screen. Select the Add-ons menu.

Step 4: Get Add-ons
After selecting the Add-ons menu, scroll down in the menu to Get Add-ons and select it.

Step 5: Search for Pear Deck
In the Google Workspace Marketplace, search for Pear Deck for Google Slides Add-on in the App search bar.

Step 6: Select Pear Deck Add-on
Choose the yellow tile titled Pear Deck for Google Slides

Step 7: Install Add-On
Select the blue Install button to begin installation of the add-on.

Step 8: Give Permission to Continue
By agreeing to the install, you are giving access to some of your files to Pear Deck to make changes, add interactive features, and present your slide show through their webserver as part of the features of the tool. To use Pear Deck, you must agree to this step.

Step 9: Choose your account
This is a secondary step to make sure you are connecting the correct account to Pear Deck. Please select the account you wish to connect to Pear Deck.

Step 10: Secondary Security
This is your chance to read the Privacy Policy for the app you are adding.

fingerprint These are very important decisions. Be aware of exactly what you are adding, what you are giving access to, and what they intend to do with the data.
